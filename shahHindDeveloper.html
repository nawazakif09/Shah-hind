About the Founder

Md. Shahnawaz Ahmad

Founder & Director — Shah Hind Developer Pvt. Ltd.

Md. Shahnawaz Ahmad is a distinguished veteran with a decorated career in India's premier security forces — the Border Security Force (BSF) and the National Security Guard (NSG). His years of service at the frontlines of national security have instilled in him an unshakeable discipline, strategic acumen, and a deep commitment to nation-building.

Channelling this ethos into the private sector, he founded Shah Hind Developer Private Limited with a mission to bridge the gap between skilled talent and growing enterprises in Bihar and across India. His leadership is defined by integrity, precision, and an unwavering focus on human potential.

Ex-BSF Ex-NSG Entrepreneur Bihar Workforce Specialist
